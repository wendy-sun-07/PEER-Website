Application for Mentees 2017-2018 will open on August 7th, 2017!

Apply to the PEER Mentoring Program.

The PEER Mentoring Program is specifically designed to help first-year Asian and Pacific-Islander students to adjust at Penn. As a mentee, you will be paired up with one of our upperclassmen mentors, and take part in our social, leadership, and service driven events. Aside from providing academic and occupational guidance, PEER is a place that offers love and support in all aspects of your life. We emphasize the values of community, understanding, and openness, and encourage our mentees to explore aspects of their Asian and Pacific-Islander identities.

The mentee application is comprised of 2 parts - an online form and an interview. The application can be accessed below, and it is really helpful for us getting to know you a bit. The interview will be taking place during New Student Orientation (NSO), and you will get a chance to ask us questions and get to know us a bit too! Application will open August 7th.
