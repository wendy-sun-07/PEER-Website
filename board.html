The dedicated PEER Board is working to plan retreats and bringing PEER many enriching and fun events throughout the year. Get excited for what's in store for this year!

Meet the PEER Board 2017-18

PEER Board is comprised of sophomores and junior who have been past PEER mentees. Board helps organizing social events like thanksgiving potluck, scavenger hunt, karaoke, and leadership development like teamwork and networking at Penn. In addition, board also helps coordinate the service project working in creating changes in the community. PEER Board is an integral part of the PEER family bringing everyone together!
